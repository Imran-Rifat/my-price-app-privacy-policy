Privacy Policy for MyPriceCheckerApp

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

1. Introduction

MyPriceCheckerApp (“we,” “our,” or “the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Camera Access: The app requires access to your device camera to scan barcodes. No images or video are stored unless you explicitly choose to save them.
  • Location Data: With your permission, the app collects your precise location (latitude and longitude) to provide local price comparisons and product information. This data is not stored on your device beyond a single session.
  • Device and Ad Identifiers: We use the Google Mobile Ads SDK, which may collect your device’s advertising ID and other identifiers. This is used to display and measure the performance of ads in the app.
  • Firebase Services:
    • Authentication: If you sign in, Firebase Authentication handles your account details (such as your phone number, email, or other details depending on the sign-in method you choose).
    • Cloud Firestore / Storage: Any data you choose to store (e.g., saved products) may be stored in Firebase.
    • Usage Data: We track anonymous usage data, such as your daily scan count, in Firestore to manage your scan quota.
    • Crashlytics: Anonymous crash and usage reports are collected to help us improve app stability.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable barcode scanning and provide product price comparisons.
  • To use your location to find and display product prices from nearby stores.
  • To display and measure the performance of advertisements within the app.
  • To store user preferences or saved products securely in Firebase.
  • To improve app performance and fix crashes using Crashlytics.

4. Sharing of Information

  • With Firebase: We share certain data with Firebase, a Google service, to enable core app functionality. This includes your User ID for authentication and tracking your daily scan limit.
  • With Google Mobile Ads: We share device and ad identifiers with Google Mobile Ads to display and target advertisements within the app.
  • With Price Comparison Servers: Your location, UPC code, and country code are sent to our backend server to retrieve product price information.

5. Security of Your Data

  • Your data is stored securely using Firebase’s security features.
  • We implement reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. User Rights

  • You can opt out of saving personal data in the app.
  • You may delete your account or stored data at any time via the app interface.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available in the app and on our website (if available).
